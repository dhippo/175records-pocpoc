The fine print

Tax reduction

175 records is a French non-profit (loi 1901). Where recognised as of general interest, a donation gives a 66% income-tax reduction (up to 20% of taxable income, art. 200 CGI). A tax receipt is issued.

Data protection

Your data (name, email) is only used to manage your membership or gift and to share our nights. It is never sold. Access, rectify or delete it anytime: contact@175records.com.

100% secure payment

Payments are handled by a secure provider (HelloAsso), PCI-DSS compliant. 175 records never stores any card data.
