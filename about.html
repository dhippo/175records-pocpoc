The collective

175 records started in a Paris back room and never really left the dark.

We are a collective of DJs, producers and friends booking nights where French touch meets electro and techno — and where the room decides when it ends.

Every night gets its own world: its own poster, its own colour, its own crowd. The only constants are the star, the sound, and the rule.
